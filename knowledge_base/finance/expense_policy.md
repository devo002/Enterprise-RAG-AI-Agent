# Travel & Expense (T&E) Policy

## Overview
Employees may request reimbursement for approved business expenses incurred while performing work duties.

## Eligible expenses
- Flights, trains, and public transport for business travel
- Hotel accommodation (reasonable cost, business purpose)
- Meals during approved travel (up to a daily limit)
- Taxi/ride-share when public transport is not feasible
- Conference tickets and work-related training (pre-approved)

## Ineligible expenses
- Alcohol (not reimbursable)
- Personal entertainment or leisure activities
- Upgrades (business/first class) unless pre-approved
- Expenses without a receipt (exceptions require manager approval)

## Approval rules
- Expenses above **$100** require manager approval.
- International travel requires manager approval before booking.

## Receipts and submission
- Receipts are required for any expense over **$25**.
- Submit reimbursements within **30 days** of the expense date.

## Payment timeline
- Approved reimbursements are paid within **10 business days**.
